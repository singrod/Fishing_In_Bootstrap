Fishing Hot Spots

Fishing is off the hook!

Florida has nearly 1200 coastline miles of saltwater fishing and thousands of freshwater lakes. If you're visiting Orlando, there are several spots you may find exciting.


Atlantic Shoreline


7 foot rod or larger.

Use 20-40 pound test.

Live shrimp or small bait fish are best.

The Gulf of Mexico


Great place to catch Gag Grouper.

Live bait fish is best.

Fish are best found near or under structures.

The Flats


Best place to catch Red Drum and Sea Trout.

Many artificial baits work great in the flats.

Use light tackle for best action.
